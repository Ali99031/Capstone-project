# Capstone-project
Introduction
The Australian car market features a wide range of variables that affect car prices, making it crucial for dealerships to use data-driven methods when setting their prices. Understanding the market dynamics can offer valuable insights that help businesses optimize their strategies, whether it's establishing competitive prices, managing depreciation, or forecasting future trends. 

Scenario
Nick, 25 years old, is looking for a car that will last for at least 5 years. He is married but has no kids, his monthly income is $20K, with no budget constraints. He is interested in a car that fits well with his lifestyle, which might involve city driving and occasional weekend getaways.

Objectives
•	What is the AVG cars by car age?
•	AVG price per status?
•	Number of cars per Fuel type
•	Number of cars by gear box?
•	Top 5 Cars Types of cars by AVG price?
•	Number of cars by Model?
Aim
To analyze Australian market related to car data ..

Target Audience
Aspirational buyers and sellers
